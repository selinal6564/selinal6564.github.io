Movie Webpage Project

Context: We used HTML and CSS to create a movie webpage.

Content: We made a movie webpage about how a cyborg gets released and goes on to cause problems and terror. This causes everyone to find ways to stop the cyborg.

Reflection: We had challenges along the way while making the webpage. One of the challenges included changing the color of the submit button. Since our background was black, we had to change the color of the submit button for it to be visible. We later realize that we had to add a class in index.html and change the background-color in style.css.

From this challenge, I realized that collaboration and communication was very important because it was very important to communicate about our ideas. It was also important to pay attention to details of the codes.

I look forward to continuing to learn more codes and website!
